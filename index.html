introduction

This project aims to visualize the detailed information about China automobile market and holdings, ranging from its development process, the latest segment market situation, to the newest sales of major automobile producers. Besides, it also provides viewers the changing trend of China car market and help investors (if any) to understand:

  • Which province holds the most cars?
  • Which segment market was in the fastest increase?
  • Which automobile producer was the most popular?

    • Objective

    In order to provide enough useful information which was carried by suitable visualizing methods, three graphs and tables were planned to draw, and their expressions are: car holding quantity and GDP of each province from 2000 to 2014, sales trend of major car producers from 2006 to 2014, and segment market and sub-brands sales of major producers from 2006 to 2014 respectively.
